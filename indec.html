🌸 PyLadies Rivers Empowering Women Through Python Welcome to PyLadies Rivers, the official PyLadies chapter serving Rivers State, Nigeria. PyLadies Rivers is a welcoming community dedicated to helping women and underrepresented groups learn Python, build technical skills, grow their careers, and connect with other members of the technology community. Whether you are a complete beginner, a student, a career changer, or an experienced developer, there is a place for you here. --- Our Mission Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where people can: - Learn Python in a beginner-friendly way. - Receive mentorship and guidance. - Collaborate on projects and open-source contributions. - Attend workshops, meetups, and community events. - Build confidence and grow professionally in technology. We believe that access to knowledge, community support, and practical experience can open doors to new opportunities. --- What We Do 🐍 Python Training We organize structured Python training programs designed for beginners and aspiring developers. Our sessions combine live instruction, practical exercises, assignments, and collaborative learning. 👩🏽‍🏫 Mentorship Members receive support from mentors and experienced community members who are passionate about helping others succeed in their learning journey. 🎉 Community Events We host meetups, workshops, study sessions, and networking events that encourage learning, sharing, and collaboration. 💻 Open Source & Projects We encourage members to explore open-source projects, contribute to the community, and build real-world applications using Python and related technologies. --- Current Program July/August 2026 Python Training Cohort Our current cohort is focused on building a strong foundation in Python programming through weekly live sessions, hands-on practice, quizzes, assignments, and mentorship. Participants are learning topics such as: - Python fundamentals - Variables and data types - Conditional statements - Loops - Functions - Data structures - File handling - Object-oriented programming - Building beginner-friendly projects --- Who Can Join? PyLadies Rivers welcomes: - Beginners with no programming experience - Students - Career changers - Professionals interested in Python - Women and underrepresented groups in technology - Allies who support our mission and community values No prior coding experience is required to get started. --- Join the Community We would love to have you as part of the PyLadies Rivers community. - Participate in our training programs. - Attend our events and workshops. - Connect with mentors and fellow learners. - Share your progress and celebrate your achievements. Together, we are building a supportive Python community in Rivers State and beyond. --- About PyLadies PyLadies is an international mentorship group with a mission to promote, educate, and advance a diverse Python community through outreach, education, conferences, events, and social gatherings. PyLadies Rivers is proud to be part of this global network. --- 🌸 PyLadies Rivers Learn Python. Build Confidence. Grow Together.
